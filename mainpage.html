Our Mission

Neuro Sketch is detection of neurological disorders through an AI-based system that uses multiple motor and cognitive tests, making healthcare more accessible, cost-effective, and non-invasive for timely diagnosis and intervention.

About Us

Welcome to Neuro Sketch, your gateway to proactive neurological health. Our app helps you uncover potential neurological risks with easy, motor-based tests right from your device. Neuro Sketch uses advanced AI to analyze motor functions and detect early signs of disorders. With just a few taps, get clarity and confidence about your brain health, anytime, anywhere. Accessible, reliable, and powered by AI—your journey to better neurological care begins here.

Our Vision

The vision of Neuro Sketch is to develop an advanced AI-driven healthcare system that provides reliable neurological disorder detection, even for remote populations, without the need for costly wearable devices. It focuses on using integrated AI tests for better health outcomes.
